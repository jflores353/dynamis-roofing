Our Process (Start - Finish)

  • Perform Inspection:

    Our professional roofing experts will assess your roof’s condition, examine all its components, and prepare a report that documents everything.

  • Provide Solution:

    After analyzing all options, we will provide you with a cost-effective proposal and recommend the ideal roofing solution for your property based on its condition.

  • Gain Approval:

    Once the proposed solution is approved, we will begin the material procurement process and order all materials necessary to initiate the job. We will inform you of when the material will be delivered to you and schedule our repairs accordingly.

  • Begin Repairs:

    • Tear off the existing roofing system
    • Inspect the wooden deck for rotting and replace it as needed.
    • Dry in with roofing material.
    • Install the final roof system (Shingle, tile, metal, etc. Will vary based on your needs.)

  • Close Job:

    Submit invoice for final payment and register warranties.

Residential Roof System

  1. Asphalt Shingle

    Asphalt shingles are one of the most common and budget-friendly roofing systems in the United States, ideal for a complete roof replacement. They come in many colors and can always be replaced after 10- 15 years. Being the most popular roof system out here in Austin, Central Texas, we have extensive experience with shingle roofs, so you can count on us to replace yours.

    2. Pros to a shingle roof:

    • - Most affordable roof system
    • - Easy to remove and replace
    • - Low maintenance
    • - Available in a wide range of colors and styles

    Cons to a Shingle Roof:

    • - Lower resistance to algae
    • - Replacement needed every 10-15 years
    • - Less likely to hold up to hail or high winds

  • Tile Roof

    One of the most beautiful roof systems available, tile roofs will last 100 years when properly maintained. They are known for being durable and heavy, but that is expected considering their century-long lifespan. While they are expensive, having them installed eliminates the inconvenience of replacing your roof every 10-15 years. They are far more capable of withstanding hail and wind than asphalt shingle systems.

    • Pros of Tile Roof:

    • - Long-lasting
    • - Energy-efficient
    • - Low maintenance Needs
    • - Good for resale value

    Cons of Tile Roof:

    • - Weighs more than your typical roof system
    • - Can be damaged from fallen trees or other impacts
    • - Requires expert installation, maintenance, and repairs

  • Metal

    Metal roofs with modern designs are growing increasingly popular in the real estate market. More people want their houses to have that distinctive black-and-white look. This increase in popularity has led to an abundance of options for metal roofs, which are a great way to add style and flair to your Central Texas home. Austin, in particular, is seeing metal roofs being installed more and more frequently due to their long lifespan of 40-70 years.

    • Pros of Metal Roof:

    • - Resistance to corrosion and cracking
    • - Extremely long lifespans
    • - Wind-resistant roofing material
    • - Streamlined aesthetic

    Cons of Metal Roof:

    • - High upfront costs compared to other roofing materials
    • - Noise during rain or hailstorms
    • - Risk of denting, fading, staining, or rusting
    • - Difficult to modify and repair
    • - Slippery and hot surface
